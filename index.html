Passion Pods

Meeting people and deepening our understanding by sharing what we learn.

While our lives are more restricted to home, possibilities are expanding out in the connected world of the Internet. However, taking the old and moving it to a new context will seldom be desired. Passion Pods is a response to reconsider how we learn, build relationships and find support for projects that are meaningful to us. We believe that learning is, and should be respected, as a natural process of play. Passion Pods hope to create a space for such learning and connect people who can be of support to each other.

In this format, a host will open a session and share what they find exciting at the moment. Each session is limited to one host and four participants. Following the sharing of the host, the participants are invited to share their thoughts and ideas on the topic.

Sign up for a session below by sending the host an e-mail, or learn to host a session by contacting our team.
